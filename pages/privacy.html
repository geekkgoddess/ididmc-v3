Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

I Did My Chores ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Joanna Hodge, She Got Sheets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our web application at ididmc.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use I Did My Chores:

  • Account information: Email address and password (or Google account details) when you sign up as a parent.
  • Household data: Household name, pay period settings, earning caps, and approval preferences you configure.
  • Kid profiles: Names, PIN codes, and avatar colors you create for your children. We do not collect any personal information directly from children.
  • Chore submissions: Chore names, dollar amounts, submission dates, and photo proof images uploaded by household members.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information through Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain your household account and chore tracking data
  • To send email notifications about chore submissions (if enabled)
  • To process subscription payments through Stripe
  • To respond to contact form inquiries
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Children's Privacy

I Did My Chores is designed for parents to manage. Children in the app interact only through a PIN-based login on a shared household link. We do not require children to create accounts, provide email addresses, or submit any personal information beyond what their parent enters on their behalf (name, PIN, avatar color). We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

Photo submissions uploaded by children are stored in your household's Firebase Firestore and are accessible only to authenticated members of your household.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), which provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Payment information is processed by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

Access to household data is restricted to authenticated parent accounts. Kid access is limited to PIN-verified sessions on the household link.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Firebase/Google: For data storage and authentication
  • Stripe: For payment processing (paid plans only)
  • Netlify: For form submissions and hosting
  • As required by law or to protect our legal rights

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at jmhodgefl@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at jmhodgefl@gmail.com.

8. Cookies

We use local storage (not traditional cookies) to save your theme preference and session state. No advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels are used.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
