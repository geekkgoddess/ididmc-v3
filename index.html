From Chore Wars to Cooperation — In 4 Steps

1

Setup (You)

Add kids with PINs, build your chore list with dollar amounts, and set what "clean" means for each task.

Your standards are defined before conflict starts.
2

Claim (Kids)

Kids log in with their PIN, pick available chores, snap a photo, and submit. First to claim a shared chore wins it.

They learn to document their work — like a real job.
3

Approve (You)

Review the photo. Approve, reject, or mark it "Exceeded Expectations." Add a bonus for standout work.

Real feedback + recognition. Not just money.
4

Payday (Both)

Balances track automatically against your pay period cap. Payouts on your schedule.

Kids experience a real paycheck. You see actual behavior change.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my kids actually use this?▼
Most kids start using it immediately because they see real money on a real timeline. It's not a punishment tool — it's a job. The app is designed to feel like a real employer, and kids respond to that.
What if they don't finish a chore?▼
You reject the photo. The chore stays available. No payment. Kids learn accountability without argument. No nagging required — the app is the "bad guy," not you.
Can I adjust chore amounts anytime?▼
Yes — anytime from your settings. Change amounts, add chores, adjust standards. Updates go live instantly for everyone in your household.
Is this just for summer, or year-round?▼
You decide. Some families use it year-round with lower amounts during the school year. Others go seasonal. It's fully flexible to your household's rhythm.
Can multiple kids share a chore?▼
Yes. First to claim locks it. Prevents double-work and teaches kids about fairness and speed. They compete for high-value chores, which drives engagement.
Do my kids need their own accounts?▼
No. Kids log in with a 4-digit PIN on a shared household link — no email, no account, no app store download. Parents manage everything. You can reset PINs anytime from your dashboard.
Is my family's data private?▼
Yes. Your household data is only accessible to authenticated members of your account. We never sell or share your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
