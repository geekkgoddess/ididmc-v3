Daily Workflow

Parents create the household, add kids and chores, share the kid dashboard link, review submissions, and record pay out at the end of the pay period.

Kids open the shared link on their device, choose their name, enter their PIN, tap chores, add proof when asked, and wait for parent approval.

Approvals

What is this?

The Approvals section shows all chore submissions from your kids that are waiting for your review.

What should I see?

Each submission displays:

• Child's name
• Chore completed
• Photo evidence (if required)
• Buttons to approve or reject
• Timestamp when submitted

What happens next?

Click "Approve" to award the points to the child. Click "Reject" to send it back if it doesn't meet your standards.

Compensation Model

Choose how your kids earn money for completing chores. You can change models at any time.

Model A — Points

Each chore is worth a set number of points. You control how many points equal $1.00. Great for households that want flexibility and bonuses.

Model B — Flat Pay

Each chore pays a set dollar amount. Simple and straightforward — kids know exactly what they'll earn before they start.

Model C — Daily Minimum

All chores count equally. If a kid completes the required number of chores, they earn their full day's pay. Rewards consistency over cherry-picking.

Approval Mode

What does this control?

How chore submissions are handled when kids complete their work.

Manual Approval

You review every submission and its photo before the kid gets credit. This gives you full control and ensures quality work.

Auto Approve

Submissions are automatically approved when submitted. Kids get credit immediately. Use this for chores you trust are done correctly.

Tip: Use Manual Approval for important chores and Auto Approve for routine ones to save time.

Kids

What is this?

Manage your children and their login credentials for the app.

What can I do here?

• See each child's name and PIN
• Add new children to your household
• Reset a child's PIN if they forget it

How do kids log in?

Kids use their PIN (4 digits) to log into the kid dashboard. They don't need passwords or email addresses.

Share with Kids

What is this?

Generate and share your household's kid dashboard link so your children can access it.

How to use it:

• Click "Copy Kid Dashboard Link"
• Paste the link in a text, email, or chat
• Kids can open it in any browser
• They'll need their PIN to log in

Where's the Household ID?

The Household ID is embedded in the dashboard link. You don't need to share it separately—just share the full link.
